About the School

Bais City National Science High School (BCNSHS) is a specialized public secondary school committed to developing academically competent, responsible, and innovative learners. It serves as a center for science, technology, and research-based education in Bais City, providing opportunities for students to excel in both academics and character.

Grades: 7 – 12

Focus: Science · Technology · Values

History

BCNSHS was established to expand access to specialized science education for promising learners of Bais City and nearby communities. Since its founding, the school has strengthened laboratory resources, upgraded teacher qualifications, and fostered partnerships with local stakeholders to support education and research excellence.

Vision

We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation.

As a learner-centered public institution, the Department of Education continuously improves itself to better serve its stakeholders.

Mission

To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where:

  • Students learn in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment.
  • Teachers facilitate learning and nurture every learner.
  • Administrators and staff ensure an enabling and supportive environment for learning.
  • Families and stakeholders actively engage and share responsibility for lifelong learners.
